Why you are going to love this!

  1. Fun.Taco Bowls are a fun and different way to enjoy a taco.
    Tacos were pretty much a weekly menu item when I was a kid. My mom made them with ground beef and served them
    in those crispy taco shells.
  2. Delicious.So many good flavors and textures all in one place
    Just mix it up and enjoy!
  3. Easy.They are easy to put together and It is a great way to
    use up leftover meat you may have on hand.
  4. Customizable.Everyone can create their own combination
    using their favorite toppings.
  5. Meal Prepping.Because everything can be prepped ahead, it’s
    the perfect make ahead meal.

What is in a Taco Bowl?

  1. Meat.For today’s version I used ground beef cooked with taco
    seasoning. You could also use ground chicken or ground turkey.
  2. Taco Seasoning.You can use store bought seasoning but it’s easy
    to make a homemade taco seasoning using your own using garlic
    powder, onion power, chili powder, cumin, oregano, and paprika.
    I’ve got a recipe down below for you.
  3. Rice.Use brown rice or white rice, whatever your preference is.
  4. Toppings.This is where you can go crazy and add your favorite
    taco toppings.

How to Make a Taco Bowl.

  1. Rice.Start with a base of brown rice. You can use white rice
    or even Mexican rice. Follow my directions for making
    perfect Instant Pot brown rice every time. We like to use the
    rice warm. You can also substitute quinoa for rice if you
    prefer or use cauliflower rice.
  2. Meat.For beef taco meat, brown hamburger and add in taco
    seasoning ingredients or a store bought taco seasoning.
    Simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add your meat on top of the rice.
  4. Beans.Drain and rinse your black beans. You can also use
    pinto beans or refried beans.
  5. Corn.I usually used canned corn but you can also use corn
    cut fresh off the cob if it is in season. Drain the canned corn.
    To give the corn a bit more flavor char it in a cast iron pan for
    a few minutes before adding it to your bowl.
  6. Toppings.Add your toppings from the list below.

Taco Bowl Favorite Toppings.

  1. Cheese.We like grated cheddar cheese but you can also use
    a Mexican blend, Monterey Jack or even a Pepper Jack cheese
    for a little kick.
  2. Lettuce.Romaine lettuce works well or even some iceberg. I
    like a lettuce with a little crunch to it. It adds nice texture to
    the bowl
  3. Salsa or Pico de gallo.You could also use some chopped
    tomatoes.
  4. Guacamole.Sometimes I just add slices of avocado too.
  5. Cilantro.Garnish the top of everything with some freshly
    chopped cilantro.
  6. Taco sauce, hot sauce or ketchup.Drizzle this over the top.
    As a kid I always had to have ketchup on my tacos!
  7. Tortilla Chips.You can add some tortilla chips too if you like
    for a little extra crunch.
  8. Sour Cream
  9. Red Onions
  10. Tomatoes
